I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student at Institute of Science and Technology Austria (ISTA), fortunate to be advised by Krishnendu Chatterjee. Prior to ISTA, I received my B.Sc. in Computer Engineering at Sharif University of Technology.

My research focus is theoretical computer science in general. In particular, I am interested in mult-agent aritifical intelligence and decision-making under uncertainty.
